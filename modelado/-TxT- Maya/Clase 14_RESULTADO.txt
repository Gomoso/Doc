SUBDIVISION SURFACES
Advantages of subdivision surfaces
    * Subdivision surfaces allow higher level control over shape than polygons.
    * They allow you to only use complex geometry in the complex regions of your model.
    * They allow creases (sharp edges) and arbitrary topology (not just four-sided sheets).
    * The continuity of subdivision surfaces eliminates many of the problems that can occur at seams when you animate NURBS surfaces.
    * You can bind subdivision surfaces to skeletons at a coarse level and the effects will translate smoothly to the finer levels.

Crear una subdivision surface.
	Create > Subdiv primitives.

Otra forma de hacerlo es convirtiendo una geometria base. 
	Modify > Convert > Poligons to Subdiv.
	Modify > Convert > NURBS to Subdiv.

Manejo Modo Poly.
	1. En Modo poly > componentes > Face.
	2. Manipulo con las herramientas de Polys.
	
Manejo de resolucion.
	1. En Modo componentes > Face.
	2. Selecciono la cara que deseo refinar.
	3. Modo componentes > Refine selected.

Para ver los diferentes niveles de resolucion.
	1. En Modo Componentes > Face.
	2. Para ver los de mayor resolucion.
		Modo componentes > Display Finer.
	3. Para ver los de menor resolucion.
		Modo componentes > Display Coarser.
